# EA-007 — Organic Visibility Campaign

## Objective

Increase the online visibility of EA Eventos through consistent organic content publishing on Facebook and Instagram.

This campaign focuses on audience growth, brand awareness and content consistency rather than paid advertising.

---

# Business Context

Current digital presence:

| Metric                  | Value |
| ----------------------- | ----- |
| Facebook Followers      | 20    |
| Instagram Followers     | 0     |
| Average Reach per Post  | 10    |
| Paid Advertising Budget | None  |

EA Eventos is entering its high-demand season, creating an opportunity to generate and publish additional content from upcoming events.

---

## Primary Goal

Increase brand visibility.
