# EA-006 — Editorial Calendar

## Objective

Create a structured publication schedule for EA Eventos that enables consistent social media activity while maximizing the use of available content assets.

The calendar serves as the operational tool connecting content strategy, content production and publication activities.

---

# Business Context

EA Eventos is entering its high-demand season, with additional events expected throughout the quarter.

Because new content will continue to be generated, the publication strategy focuses on maintaining a consistent presence while allowing future assets to enter the content pipeline.

Current channels:

* Facebook
* Instagram

Future channels may include:

* Wedding directories
* Event vendor platforms
* Additional social media platforms

---

# Publication Strategy

## Feed Content

Recommended frequency:

* 3 publications per week.

Primary objectives:

* Showcase event atmosphere.
* Promote available services.
* Generate inquiries and quotations.

Content formats:

* Reels
* Posts
* Photo publications

---

## Stories

Stories may be published more frequently depending on content availability.

Objectives:

* Increase visibility.
* Maintain audience engagement.
* Reuse existing assets.
* Support active promotions.

---

# Content Sources

Content used in the calendar originates from the asset management process established in previous phases.

Workflow:

Content Audit
↓
Asset Classification
↓
Metadata Assignment
↓
Priority Selection
↓
Content Calendar
↓
Publication

---

# Asset Prioritization Rules

## High Priority Assets

Usage:

* Immediate publication.
* Core content strategy.
* Promotional campaigns.

---

## Medium Priority Assets

Usage:

* Content reserve.
* Secondary publications.
* Future campaign support.

---

## Low Priority Assets

Usage:

* Archive.
* Future review.
* Emergency content reserve.

---

# Calendar Structure

The publication calendar includes the following fields:

| Field        | Description                      |
| ------------ | -------------------------------- |
| Date         | Scheduled publication date       |
| Platform     | Publication channel              |
| Content Type | Reel, Post, Story, etc.          |
| Asset        | Assigned content asset           |
| Objective    | Purpose of the publication       |
| Notes        | Production or publication status |

---

# Content Distribution

The calendar follows the content strategy established in EA-005.

Distribution:

* 70% Events in Action
* 30% Service Promotion

This balance allows EA Eventos to demonstrate event results while maintaining commercial visibility.

---

# Calendar Maintenance Process

The editorial calendar is a living document.

After every event:

1. Capture content.
2. Audit assets.
3. Classify content.
4. Assign metadata.
5. Prioritize assets.
6. Update content pool.
7. Update publication calendar.

---

# Expected Benefits

Implementation of the editorial calendar provides:

* Consistent social media activity.
* Better use of available content.
* Improved publication planning.
* Reduced content shortages.
* Better alignment between marketing and operations.

---

# Key Outcome

EA Eventos now has a documented editorial planning process that transforms raw event footage into scheduled marketing content.

The calendar provides a repeatable framework that can scale as additional events and content become available.
