# EA-005 — Content Strategy

## Objective

Define a sustainable content strategy for EA Eventos that supports business growth, increases brand visibility and improves future content generation.

The strategy focuses on maximizing the value of available content while establishing a repeatable process for future events.

---

# Business Context

EA Eventos provides entertainment and event production services for social and corporate events.

Current services include:

* DJ
* Cabina
* Iluminación
* Pantalla de 55"
* Máquina de humo
* Micrófono
* Cañones LED
* Paquete Carnaval (subject to availability)

Target event types:

* Bodas
* XV Años
* Cumpleaños
* Eventos corporativos

Current marketing channels:

* Facebook
* Instagram

Potential future channels:

* Wedding directories
* Event vendor platforms
* Additional social media platforms

---

# Strategic Findings

The content audit identified that EA Eventos generates valuable experiences during events but lacks a structured content generation process.

Current available content mainly demonstrates:

* Event atmosphere
* Audience engagement
* Lighting effects
* Entertainment experience

Current content does not adequately demonstrate:

* Event setup process
* Professional equipment
* Technical capabilities
* Client testimonials
* Operational workflow

As a result, the primary limitation is not the lack of events but the lack of a structured content capture system.

---

# Strategic Objectives

The content strategy aims to:

1. Increase visibility on social media.
2. Build trust with potential customers.
3. Showcase available services.
4. Generate inquiries and quotations.
5. Create a repeatable content generation process.
6. Improve content quality in future events.

# Content Capture System

To improve future marketing efforts, every event should generate content from three stages.

## Before the Event

Capture:

* Arrival.
* Equipment unloading.
* Setup process.
* Equipment preparation.

---

## During the Event

Capture:

* Crowd engagement.
* Lighting effects.
* DJ operation.
* General atmosphere.

---

## After the Event

Capture:

* Final venue overview.
* Equipment teardown.
* Event summary shots.
